Weddings's Photography

Nowadays, wedding photography is not limited to taking posed pictures of the bride and groom, random clicks of the rituals or some emotionless shots of the relatives huddled together. Candid photography and properly framed and timed shots ensure that the picture portrays the energy and emotions of the moment in full authenticity. This is where "Shutter Stories" comes into picture... Literally!
At Shutter Stories, we like to call ourselves "Dreamweavers". We are trained artists and visualizers adept in bringing thoughts & emotions to a tangible reality.     Read More...

Pre Weddings Photography

Pre wedding shoots are offered as a great way for us to get to know each other better before the wedding day. We spend time together, discuss the wedding plans and have fun together. We get to experience you as a couple and you get to experience a professional photographer taking your photographs and get a feel for how we work. This activity gives you the credence we are looking for. On your wedding day we are more of friends spending the time with you rather than a stranger roaming around in the background.     Read More...
